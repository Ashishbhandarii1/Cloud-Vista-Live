About Us

A Home Away From Home

Nestled in the tranquil hills of Narendra Nagar, Artist Rally Homestay offers an authentic mountain experience just minutes from the spiritual heart of Rishikesh. Our family has been welcoming guests for over 8 years, sharing our culture, cuisine, and love for these magnificent mountains.

Whether you're seeking adventure along the Ganges, spiritual solace at ancient temples, or simply a peaceful retreat, we provide the perfect base for your Himalayan journey.
